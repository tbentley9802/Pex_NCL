      Why Host your Next Event AboardA Norwegian Cruise Line Ship

      Provide a New Experience

      What’s more unique and extraordinary than a moving venue with stunning ever-changing views? Then you add all the different options for work and play — there’s nothing like it!

      A Planner's Dream

      The value and convenience of included meeting spaces, entertainment, dining and more help alleviate many pain points of the planning process.

      Freestyle Dining

      With no fixed dining times or pre-assigned seating arrangements, attendees can dine with freedom and flexibility. Plus, Norwegian offers more dining choices than any other cruise line, including complimentary dining rooms, 24-hour restaurants, casual buffets and specialty restaurants.

      Complimentary Entertainment

      Let us entertain your whole group. Whether they want to take in a spectacular Broadway show, put on their poker face at our casino, laugh it up at a comedy club or dance the night away under the stars, there’s something for everyone. And it’s all included.

      Innovative Spaces

      Our complimentary meeting spaces include cutting-edge multimedia capabilities and a highly capable support staff. All event spaces are designed to elevate and accentuate whatever function you have planned.

      Wide Range of Accommodations

      Choose from a wide range of contemporary and comfortable accommodations, from the exclusive and luxurious the Haven by Norwegian to our award-winning Studios. Wherever you like to unwind, we have you covered — and well within your budget.

      THE ONBOARD EXPERIENCE

      Dine with the freedom and flexibility of Freestyle Dining, a revolutionary concept of no fixed dining times or pre-assigned seating and more dining choices than any other cruise line. Our newest ships take Freestyle Dining to the next level with new dining experiences along The Waterfront, specially curated dishes created by celebrity chefs, never-before-seen dining venues and more. Simply put, we do dining unlike anyone else at sea.

      THE ONBOARD EXPERIENCE

      Our ships feature a wide variety of bars and lounges, each with its own unique vibe. Dance to the ultraviolet energy at Bliss Ultra Lounge. Sip champagne, sake or a hand-crafted cocktail. Lounge poolside with an umbrella drink. Play pool at the pub. Or chill at the Ice Bar. Follow your mood and mingle.

      THE ONBOARD EXPERIENCE

      Our ships are known for award-winning entertainment beyond anything at sea. And the best part is that most shows and parties are complimentary, bringing smiles to your attendees and your budget.
