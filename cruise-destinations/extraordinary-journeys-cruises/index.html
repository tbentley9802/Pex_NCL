    Hop continents on an immersive, unforgettable repositioning cruise with Norwegian Cruise Line.

    It’s the rush of excitement you will feel as you uncover the mysteries of your travels. It’s the delight in learning the proper way to eat gelato in Italy – for breakfast. The magic of your travels leaves an enduring mark through the memories you create and share. That’s what makes the journey so extraordinary. Come aboard Norwegian Cruise Line and experience one of our uniquely designed Extraordinary Journeys that allow you more immersive experiences and discover how the journey can change you.

    Immerse Yourself in Extraordinary Experiences

    • Calling All Sun Worshippers
    • Cruise into the Past
    • Go a Little Wild
    • Savor Every Moment

    Feel the warm sand on your skin and let the gentle sounds of waves lull you into a blissful afternoon nap while lounging on Bora Bora’s beautiful Matira Beach. Or dive into the Maldives' pristine waters and wave to a passing whale shark. Pufferfish, lionfish, eagle rays and reef sharks swim by in blurs of color as you snorkel or scuba dive the country’s world-famous Ari Atoll and then recline beachside with a celebratory handcrafted cocktail. Revel in breathtaking ocean views and relaxation as you explore some of the world’s best beaches with Norwegian.

    Embrace your inner explorer and contemplate the mysteries surrounding the well-preserved walls and terraces of Machu Picchu. Breathe in the fresh Peruvian Andes air and journey back in time to the mighty Inca Empire while touring one of the Wonders of the World. Or cruise even farther back in time while strolling along the Valley of Kings, the resting place of pharaohs and one of the most important archaeological sites in the world. Come discover beauty and inspiration on an Extraordinary Journey with Norwegian.

    Imagine staring in awe as elephants leisurely cross the road in front of your open-air 4x4 vehicle or the rush of adrenaline you’ll feel watching a pride of lions on the prowl during your safari at the Kwantu Private Game Reserve. Be amazed witnessing a humpback whale breach the water against the backdrop of Alaska’s immense glaciers and rugged mountain peaks. Hear the distant rumble of towering waterfalls and songbirds calling to one another as you hike through Kauai’s spectacular tropical rainforest. Uncover dramatic vistas and exotic wildlife on an Extraordinary Journey.

    Chopsticks in hand, select your next slice of fresh sushi or twirl handmade ramen noodles from a steaming bowl of broth while sampling some of Tokyo’s legendary cuisine. Colorfully dressed hula dancers move in rhythm to traditional Hawaiian music as you enjoy poke, poi and other delicious dishes at a Maui beachside luau. Between touring Rome’s famous museums and ancient ruins, settle in at a local eatery with traditional carbonara followed by a decadent scoop (or two) of gelato. Enjoy the freedom and flexibility to savor regional dishes and dive even deeper into local cultures and traditions.

    Remarkable Port CitiesExtraordinary Journeys

    Cruises to Santorini, Greece

    The whitewashed towns of Oia and Fira offer breathtaking views of the caldera below. Savor the Mediterranean cuisine and a chilled glass of Santorini wine, the product of the rich volcanic soil.
    EXPLORE

    Cruises to Port Victoria, Seychelles

    Gorgeous and romantic, the Seychelles define tropical beauty. Located on Mahé Island in the Indian Ocean, this area is famous for idyllic beaches and scenic national parks, which are perfect for hiking to get the island’s best views.
    EXPLORE

    Cruises to Safaga, Egypt

    Journey to Luxor and witness the famed Valley of the Kings where the tomb of Tutankhamun and the massive Colossi of Memnon sit. Or take a jeep safari into the desert and enjoy the dramatic beauty of the vast sand dunes.
    EXPLORE

    Cruises to Reykjavik, Iceland

    Discover this fascinating land where the forces of nature hold sway as you visit the modern capital with its thermally heated outdoor swimming pool, remarkable botanic gardens and folk museum.
    EXPLORE

    Cruises to Bora Bora, French Polynesia

    The dramatic Mount Otemanu stands watch over the sparkling turquoise waters and sugary sands of Bora Bora, known as the most romantic island in the world.
    EXPLORE

    Cruises to Lima (Callao), Peru

    Explore Lima’s wonderful museums and visit the famed Cathedral of Lima and the Archbishop’s Palace on the city’s majestic main square. Take a flight to Machu Picchu, known as the “Lost City of the Incas”.
    EXPLORE

    Cruises to Skagway, Alaska

    Take a walking tour of this well-preserved gold rush town or jump aboard the White Pass and Yukon Route Railroad to witness the trail gold rushers etched for themselves.
    EXPLORE

    Cruises to Richard’s Bay, South Africa

    Situated on the deepest natural harbor in the Southern Hemisphere, Richard’s Bay will thrill both nature and culture seekers. Spend the day on a safari tour at one of the many nature reserves in the area.
