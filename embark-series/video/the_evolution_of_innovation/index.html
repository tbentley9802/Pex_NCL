Embark Spotlight

The Evolution of Innovation

In this three-part episode of EMBARK with NCL, dive into the company’s ground-breaking history, NCL’s revolutionary impact on the cruising industry, its world-class onboard entertainment and a first-look at the brand’s newest ship – Norwegian Prima.
  • The History of Innovation

    In the first episode, follow along as Cruise Director Simon Akinwolere guides you through NCL’s 55-year ground-breaking history, its legacy of firsts and how the brand revolutionized cruising forever.

  • The Evolution of Entertainment

    In part two of The Evolution of Innovation, we pull back the curtain to NCL’s entertainment at sea, and how it was a pioneer in bringing Broadway-caliber talent and productions on board across its fleet. From Tony Award®-winning musicals and show-stopping performances, see how NCL was the first to premiere “SIX – The Musical” before its debut on Broadway.

  • The Future of the Cruise Experience

    Part three of The Evolution of Innovation showcases the brand’s commitment to pioneering new guest experiences, from the development of NCL’s state-of-the-art PortMiami terminal named “the Pearl of Miami” to the soon-to-debut Norwegian Prima, the first in the Prima Class - a new class of ships in nearly a decade. The brand is elevating everything aboard its newest ship by pushing boundaries in space, service, design, and a variety of experiences that is unparalleled in the industry.
