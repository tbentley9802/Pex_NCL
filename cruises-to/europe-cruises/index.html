    Europe's Leading Cruise Line, 14 years in a row.

    There's no better way to experience the history, landmarks and culture of Europe than with our European cruise vacations. Step on board one of our award winning ships embarking from fifteen convenient cities that are amazing destinations within themselves. Unpack once and wake up to a new city every day while exploring the sights, sounds and flavors of Europe, Scandinavia & Baltic, the Mediterranean, Greece, or an Italian cruise on your own schedule. Combine that with spectacular onboard entertainment, luxurious spa services, and no set dining times, and you’re bound for a good time. It's no wonder we are the World Travel Award winner for "Europe's Leading Cruise Line" 14 years in a row.

     

    Europe CruisesThe Best of Both Worlds

      Explore OurEUROPE CRUISES

      Mediterranean Cruises

      See the Mediterranean just the way you like. Relax on your balcony and enjoy the panoramic views of the soaring cliffs along the Amalfi Coast, where Aegean sailors journeyed and trade flourished. Enjoy a wide variety of itineraries: Greek Isles cruises that will leave you enchanted, to more immersive Mediterranean journeys that will allow you to visit Spain, Greece, Italy and France in one grand voyage. Cruise from Barcelona, Venice or Rome.

      MEDITERRANEAN CRUISES

      Greek Isles Cruises

      Take a holiday, cruise to Greece, and experience the beauty and magnificence of the Mediterranean for an unforgettable vacation. Explore the mainland and witness ancient history, or travel to the Greek Isles for rich culture and picturesque sun-drenched beaches of the once-powerful empire. Experience lively Santorini as you stroll through its picturesque, winding streets. Explore the island of Mykonos and its famed whitewashed homes and beautiful beaches. Or lose yourself in the deep blue waters of Corfu and its collection of quaint cafes.

      GREEK ISLES CRUISES

      Northern Europe Cruises

      Sail on a Northern Europe cruise and witness the majesty and otherworldly natural beauty of this region. Immerse yourself in the history and tradition of the Baltic region while you visit imperial palaces and vivid churches. Travel through the British Isles -- from the lush scenery of Northern Ireland to the dynamic city of London. Pinch yourself as you cruise through the marvelous Norwegian Fjords and follow the path Vikings once traveled. Cruise from London, Amsterdam, Copenhagen and Stockholm and set off in the adventure of a lifetime.

      NOTHERN EUROPE CRUISES

      Remarkable Port CitiesCruises to Europe

      Cruises to Barcelona, Spain

      Visit the charming squares of the old Gothic Quarter and the Ramblas, Barcelona’s famed downtown promenade. You can’t miss Antoni Gaudi’s La Sagrada Familia and the Montjuic Gardens.
      EXPLORE

      Cruises to Venice, Italy

      Savor the majestic beauty of this amazing city as you glide along its canals, narrow alleys and under its bridges, opulent palazzi and elegant churches lining the route.
      EXPLORE

      Cruises to Santorini, Greece

      The whitewashed towns of Oia and Fira offer breathtaking views of the caldera below. Savor the Mediterranean cuisine and a chilled glass of Santorini wine, the product of the rich volcanic soil.
      EXPLORE

      Cruises to Flam, Norway

      Surrounded by steep mountains, thundering waterfalls and emerald-green valleys, Flåm clings to Europe’s longest, deepest fjord. It’s a fairy tale setting that can be enjoyed many ways.
      EXPLORE

      Cruises to Reykjavik, Iceland

      Discover this fascinating land where the forces of nature hold sway as you visit the modern capital with its thermally heated outdoor swimming pool, remarkable botanic gardens and folk museum.
      EXPLORE

      Cruises to Copenhagen, Denmark

      Visit the quaint old harbor at Nyhavn and beautiful Rosenborg Castle and, of course, the famed statue of the Little Mermaid. Or venture into the countryside and enjoy its panoramic vistas and fairy tale castles.
      EXPLORE

      Cruises to St. Petersburg, Russia

      St. Petersburg is full of history and breathtaking structures. Its onion-domed cathedrals and the immense royal buildings including the Winter Palace, now the Hermitage Museum, are a sight to behold.
      EXPLORE

      Cruises to Dubrovnik, Croatia

      Enjoy a walk through this famous walled medieval city and its exquisite Venetian monuments. See the Franciscan monastery and explore the Rector’s palace and the cathedral.
      EXPLORE

        European Cruise Packages for the Entire Family

        There are family vacations. Then there are the kinds of enriching family vacations your family will remember forever. Cruise the Mediterranean, Baltic, Greece, and British Isles with Norwegian Cruise Line and discover the ease and convenience of unpacking once and experiencing it all. Thanks to Norwegian's freedom and flexibility, guests of all sizes can experience the best vacation ever. Because, with families, it's all about options. And we've got them, from accommodations that fit all families to delicious dining choices even finicky eaters can't refuse. We haven't forgotten about teens – they also have an exclusive onboard refuge at Entourage.
