    CRUISE ENTERTAINMENT

    • EmailSign Up For Special Offers
    • PhoneLet Us Call You
    Sign up to get the hottest cruise deals!

    When it comes to the award-winning entertainment on board our ships, you won’t believe your eyes or even your ears. Journey from the gritty streets of New Jersey to glamour and international fame with Jersey Boys. Rock out to the six-time Tony Award ®winning Kinky Boots with music and lyrics by Cyndi Lauper. Put on your dancing shoes for Footloose or groove to the tunes of a New Orleans speakeasy at Happy Hour Prohibition: The Musical. From hit Broadway musicals to buzzworthy original shows, discover the best entertainment at sea with Norwegian.

      Tony Award® Winning MusicalKINKY BOOTS

      A fabulous entertainer. A factory worker down on his luck. You wouldn’t think these two would cross paths, and yet … Kinky Boots tells the heartwarming story based on true events of this unlikely partnership. With original songs by Cyndi Lauper, this Broadway sensation will have you clapping along and cheering the characters on as they work together to create something the world has never seen before. Having won every major Best Musical award, Kinky Boots sets the stage for a truly incredible evening at sea.

      Available on Norwegian Encore.

      Don't Miss A MomentBROADWAY SHOWS

      SIX

      Introducing the cultural phenomenon musical sweeping the stages and now gracing the seas aboard Norwegian Bliss and Norwegian Breakaway — Six. From Tudor Queens to Pop Princesses, the six wives of Henry VIII take to the mic to tell their tales, remixing five hundred years of historical heartbreak into a 75-minute celebration of 21st century girl power. Written by Toby Marlow and Lucy Moss, Six is the sell-out award-winning musical that has not only taken the theater world by storm but also the charts. These Queens may have green sleeves, but their lipstick is rebellious red.

      Available on Norwegian Bliss and Norwegian Breakaway.

      Pull Up A Chair AtTHE CHOIR OF MAN

      Pull up a chair at The Choir of Man and raise your glass to high energy dancing, live percussion and hit songs that flow as freely as the beer. You’ll feel right at home in The Jungle, the rockin’ English pub where the talented cast performs everything from classic rock hits and pub tunes to sing-along favorites. Expanding on some of the most compelling entertainment at sea, The Choir of Man, which is exclusively featured on Norwegian Encore and Norwegian Escape, is a guest favorite and is certain to make for a toast-worthy night.

      Available on Norwegian Encore and Norwegian Escape.

        Scorching ShowsBURN THE FLOOR

        Swiveling hips. Steamy embraces. High-octane tempos. The performances of Burn the Floor are not just hot – they sizzle! This ballroom and Latin dance theater company is renowned for its gifted dancers, selected from the most prestigious dance competitions around the globe. Together, they have traveled the world performing and astounding audiences in more than 300 cities in over 29 countries, including the Longacre Theater on Broadway.

        Available on Norwegian Breakaway, Norwegian Getaway & Norwegian Epic.

        Feel the VibeLIVE MUSIC

        SYD NORMAN'S POUR HOUSE

        Jam out to famous rock hits at the new Syd Norman’s Pour House where live music and an interactive cast will have you toasting the rock-and-roll legends all night long.

        Available on Norwegian Breakaway & Norwegian Getaway

        HOWL AT THE MOON

        For a big dose of feel-good, sing-along fun, nothing beats Howl at the Moon. Witness an electrifying blaze of ebony and ivory going head to head – while the audience instantly transforms into one giant happy wave of camaraderie. It’s the World’s Greatest Rock ‘n’ Roll Dueling Piano Show. It’s contagious and unlike anything else at sea.

        Available on Norwegian Breakaway, Norwegian Epic, Norwegian Getaway, Norwegian Escape.

        THE CAVERN CLUB

        Step into the legendary Liverpool club where the Beatles performed. Experience a vibrant rock-and-roll scene, featuring live music and even a Beatles cover band.

        Available on Norwegian Bliss, Norwegian Epic, Norwegian Encore and Norwegian Joy.

        Non-stop ActionLET US ENTERTAIN YOU

        LEGENDS IN CONCERT

        The longest-running tribute show in entertainment history brings the world’s greatest celebrity impersonators to the high seas. This live musical celebration features the world’s greatest celebrity tribute artists such as Lady Gaga, Adele, Tina Turner and Michael Jackson. Every performer is a perfectionist, and every performance is a show-stopper.

        Available on Norwegian Pearl.

        PRISCILLA QUEEN OF THE DESERT

        Get your glitter on with the award-winning Broadway musical Priscilla Queen of the Desert. Follow the fabulous journey of three “divas” who take their show to the Australian outback. From a dazzling array of over 500 imaginative costumes, 200 headdresses and a hit parade of dance numbers and disco hits from the 70s and 80s, you’re in for a glamorous good time.

        Available on Norwegian Epic.

        Follow Your Mood and MinglePARTIES GALORE

        Party on – day or night. Step aboard and join us on the pool deck for the sailaway party. Put on your little black dress and head over to Bliss Ultra Lounge. Or dance the night away under the stars at our H2Glow celebration or one of our many themed parties.

        Sidesplitting LaughterCOMEDY

        LEVITY ENTERTAINMENT GROUP

        Tickle your funny bone at Headliners Comedy Club on Norwegian Getaway with performances by Levity Entertainment Group’s roster of topnotch comedians. Employing more than 300 artists nationwide, Levity is the largest producer of live comedy in the United States, creating tours and TV specials for the biggest names in the industry, including Daniel Tosh, Amy Schumer, Gabriel Iglesias and Jeff Dunham. Are you ready for a sidesplitting good time?

        Available on Norwegian Getaway and Norwegian Escape.

        ExperienceEXCLUSIVE ENTERTAINMENT

        Production Shows

        The Norwegian Production Cast takes you on a high energy, full scale production show to re-live your favorite memories and music.

        Deal or No Deal

        Enjoy an exhilarating show where contestants play & deal for cash in a high-energy contest of nerves and intuition.

        Variety Shows

        From standup comedy, unicycle-riding jugglers, to our world famous “Fountains” skit you're invited for an evening of laughter.
