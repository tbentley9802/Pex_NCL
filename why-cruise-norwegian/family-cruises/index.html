    FAMILY CRUISES

    • EmailSign Up For Special Offers

    Experience an unforgettable family vacation with Norwegian Cruise Line. From dodgeball to music mania, there are lots of activities offered for every age group.

    Plus kids can choose their activity while in Splash Academy and show off their artistic skills, burn off energy in the dynamic zone, or relax in the chill zone. Teens can enjoy the coolest themed parties and gaming.

    These are the moments you’ll remember. Cruise Norwegian and see why we were voted Best Family Experience by Travel Weekly.

    Guppies Nursery is permanently closed on Norwegian Escape.
    Operational hours are subject to change without advance notice per shipboard health and safety protocols.

      Take Advantage of OurYOUTH PROGRAMS

      Guppies Programs (6 months – under 3 years)

      Guppies Hosted Activities: Fleetwide for parents and babies. Guppies Open Play: Fleetwide for parents and babies.

      Temporarily Not Available

      Splash Academy (Ages 5 – 12)

      When your little ones are having a great time, you're having a great time. Sign them up for our complimentary youth program where they can enjoy creative play, sports and more. From theme night activities to cool parties, our youth staff has a knack for this stuff.

      Entourage (Ages 13 – 17)

      Run off and play while your teenagers hang out in a place filled with video games, movies, arts, music, sports and people their own age. From the coolest lounge at sea to the hippest dance clubs at night, there’s always something going on at our teen centers. Best of all, it’s all complimentary and supervised.

      Late Night/Port Play

      Drop off the kids and make it a romantic night out. Available on days in port and evenings for children ages 3 – 12, our Group Sitting is supervised by our certified youth staff. Additional charge applies.

      Temporarily Not Available

      NOTE:

      Operational hours are subject to change without advance notice per shipboard health and safety protocols.

      Relax In OurFAMILY ACCOMMODATIONS

      Read a bedtime story on your private balcony. Whether your crew is small or large, choose from multi-room Suites or interconnecting staterooms. All of our ships have Family Mini-Suites, Balconies, Oceanviews and Inside staterooms and most of our ships have 2-Bedroom Family Suites conveniently located near the kids' area. Your little ones don't have to go far to play.
      LEARN MORE

      Enjoy Norwegian ExclusiveFAMILY DINING, ENTERTAINMENT, AND ACTIVITIES

      Dining

      Sit down to a hot pizza. Check out our made-to-order buffet. Or make it a memorable night at a specialty restaurant. With Freestyle Dining, you can eat where you want, when you want. Plus, kids age 3 and under always eat for free and kids 4-12 can order from our complimentary kids’ menu.

      Activities

      From extreme to serene activities, everyone in the family can enjoy the freedom and flexibility to do their own thing, on their own time. Bowl a strike. Whoosh down a waterslide. Play a round of mini-golf or two. Bonding experiences are around every corner.
