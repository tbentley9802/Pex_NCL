    Come get your cruise on to get your game on. Norwegian’s innovative ships have taken onboard activities to new heights. Get in the fast lane on a thrilling race track, which only Norwegian offers, or hang on the largest ropes course at sea. Then slow it down, way down, with a hot stone massage at Mandara Spa. Whether you’re setting sail with friends, enjoying a romantic retreat or bringing the whole family along, the smiles come in all sizes with incredible experiences for all ages.

    The Biggest Thrills at SeaTHRILLPLEX

    Cruise into adrenaline-pumping activities in our ThrillPlex activity zones that’ll keep you on the edge of your seat. Zoom around one of the largest race tracks at sea. Discover larger-than-life adventures during an exciting game of augmented reality laser tag. Hang with friends on the largest ropes course at sea and then whoosh over the side of the ship on Ocean Loops in the Aqua Park. Or dive into a world unlike any other in the Galaxy Pavilion and immerse yourself in a virtual gaming wonderland. Get in the zone for the biggest thrills at sea with Norwegian.

      Exclusively on Breakaway and Breakaway Plus class ships, our revolutionary quarter-mile oceanfront promenade is designed to connect you with the sea. Discover a new restaurant, or simply watch the sun set with a mojito in your hands.

      Relax & RejuvenateSELF-CARE AT SEA

      10 Ways to Stay Active on your Cruise

      Even though you're going on a cruise, you don't need to skip your daily exercise. In fact, a cruise vacation is an ideal time to shake up your fitness routine by trying a new sport or incorporating an entirely new activity into the mix.

      Read More

      Relax & Rejuvenate at the Spa

      When you want some "me" time, look no further than Mandara Spa®. Relax with a hot-stone massage, one of over 50 specialty treatments offered. Let our pampering professionals soothe and rejuvenate you with facials, massages, acupuncture and a full menu of services.

      Read More

      The Perfect Escape: Spice H20

      Find a spot in the sun and enjoy your favorite book while relaxing on a chaise lounge. Or enjoy a peaceful moment under a cascading waterfall.

      Learn More

      Award-WinningCasino

      Up the ante on your “play time” at Norwegian’s award-winning casino. Come try your hand at our reel, video or hybrid slots. Pull up a chair for some video poker or at one of our table games. From poker, blackjack and Baccarat to craps and roulette, get ready for a good time with friends, both old and new. You can also show off your skills at one of the exciting tournaments offered on every cruise or reserve your spot in one of Casino’s unique special events. Casinos at Sea is available in all ships except Pride of America.

      Learn More

      Treat Yourself to SomeOnboard Shopping

      Shopping at Great Savings

      Whether you forgot something at home or are looking to treat yourself to some duty-free items, our variety of onboard shopping venues offer a large selection of options at significant savings. Our boutique-style shops are filled with renowned brands, souvenirs, clothing and sundry items. You will also find great deals on duty-free favorites like liquor and tobacco products, jewelry, perfumes, watches and much more.

      Art for Everyone

      Did you know that you could get outstanding deals on art onboard? Browse our collections featuring iconic artists like Peter Max and Romero Britto or learn about art history at one of our fascinating seminars. Looking for a little excitement? Join us for one of our electrifying art auctions: they’re fast, furious and a whole lot of fun. You may fall in love with a work of art, or win one of our thrilling grand prize raffles.
